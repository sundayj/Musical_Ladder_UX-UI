*Frequently Asked Questions:

*When you locate the question you have, simply click the question to reveal your answer.*

I'm used to using the mobile layout! Where is everything?

Show Answer

On mobile, due to the small screen size, everything has its own individual screen for easier navigation.

However, this isn't necessary on your tablet or desktop, so we've tried to lay everything out in a way that you can find what you need in a quick glance. Now, notes and tests are automatically sorted by your teachers' names and the intruments you are learning through them. Simply click on their names to see what you need!

If you'd like more direction, please see the other FAQs below!

I'm only seeing tests and notes for one of my teachers. How do I see the others?

Show Answer

Just click on the name of your other teacher and their notes and tests will show below their name.

How do I see what my teacher said in his/her note?

Show Answer

Simple! Just click on the note and its contents will open directly below.

My teacher said they sent an attachment inside their note, but I don't see it. How do I find the attachment?

Show Answer

Whenever you go to your teacher's note, you should see a paperclip, like this one . Simply click the paperclip, and your attachment should open.

How do I see the events I've been invited to?

Show Answer

In the events section, if you click the name of the month whose events you'd like to view, they will open up below the month name for you.

I see my events and their times, but where do I go for the event?

Show Answer

If you select the event, additional information should appear below. The address for the event should be found there. If not, please contact your school or the person who invited you to the event.

I don't see my question listed here. What should I do?

Show Answer

If the answer you're looking for isn't in our FAQ, then please contact your school for assistance. Their information is listed at the very top of this page.
