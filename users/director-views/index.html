Why am I seeing all of the Teachers, Parents, and Students at once when I log in?

Show Answer

The list you see is ever user in your school. If you click on their name, you can see all of the info pertaining to them in another screen.

Conversely, if you want to narrow down the list, you can start typing their name into the search box and everyone with a name containing those letters will appear in the list for you.

If you want to see only teachers, students, or parents, then simply uncheck the boxes for the groups you don't wish to see.
