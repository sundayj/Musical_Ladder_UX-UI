Currently only available for mobile. From the intially loaded screen, students can see the teachers and instruments they're taking, respectively, as well as their current level and upcoming test date for the next level for that instrument.
The help icon in the top right corner will open an overlay that gives the contact information for the students' schools as well as a key that explains what each of the icons are for.
On the test screen, students can choose to see upcoming and completed tests as well as their respective dates.
The notes screen shows the students' notes from each individual teacher.
The events screen shows the student every event they've been invited to.

Currently only available for mobile. As a parent, the initially loaded screen shows what classes you may be taking, and your children with their classes, respectively. Initially, the icons on the bottom are disabled. Once you choose a student, whether yourself or one of your children, the site will open in their respective student view. For ease of use, as a parent, the upper left corner will always have a hamburger menu that lets you either choose "Home" to return to the initial load screen, or an individual student.

Go to

Currently available for desktop view. Can be viewed on mobile, though not intended for mobile use at this time. The director has full permission to see, utilize, and edit all data through the app. This means the director can see and edit school locations, events, teachers, parents, students, and he/she can see the notes passed from teachers to students.
The director sees a calendar that lists all of the events for his/her school, as well as any current, or upcoming, events. The director also has a search bar with a filter function that allows the director to dynamically search through their entire list of current users, including teachers, parents, and students, whether there are 12 students or 1,300. If the director knows the level of the user they're searching for, they can narrow their search firther by selecting or deselecting the user checkboxes.
