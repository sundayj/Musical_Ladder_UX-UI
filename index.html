From the intially loaded screen, student can see the teachers and instruments their taking, respectively, as well as their current level and upcoming test date for their next level for that instrument.
The help icon in the top right corner will open an overlay that gives the contact information for the student's school as well a key that explains what each of the icons are for.
On the test screen, students can choose to see upcoming and completed tests as well as their respective dates.
The notes screen shows the student's notes from each individual teacher.
The events screen shows the student every event they've been invited to.

Initially, the icons on the bottom are disabled. Once you choose a student, whether yourself or one of your children, the site will open in their respective student view.
